Photography

In summer of 2017 a close friend of mine sold me his old DSLR for $100 (a bargain). It was an old 12mp Pentax camera with with only one auto focus point and a scratched up viewfinder. I played around with it for several months and learned to enjoy the process of photography. In 2018 I was awarded my first bonus at my job and decided to spend some of it on a "nice" camera. I ended up getting a Sony α6000 mirrorless camera. A huge step up from what I was using previously. This camera has been one of my best purchases. The pictures it takes are great and its the perfect size to take anywhere.
